About

Alyssa Morgan is a painter focused on light studies in oil and graphite. Her practice explores calm atmospheres, soft edges, and slow looking. Works have been exhibited across the U.S. and held in private collections.

Available for commissions and residencies. For inquiries, please email studio@example.com.
